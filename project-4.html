💼 Internship Overview

During my internship at Bluestock, I worked as a Front-End Developer and Co-Team Leader. Our task was to develop a website that mirrored an existing site provided by the company. The focus was on presenting financial details of 5-6 companies in a structured and accessible way.

🎯 Objective

The objective was to design and build a user-friendly website that clearly displays key information such as: company profits, losses, balance sheets, financial ratios, advantages, and disadvantages. It needed to be clean, responsive, and easy to navigate for end users.

👩‍💻 My Role & Responsibilities

I contributed to the front-end design and development of the site.

Managed the team workflow as a Co-Team Leader and ensured smooth collaboration across all members.

Gathered and uploaded real company financial data, ensuring accuracy and currency.

Worked on making the UI visually appealing and easy to interact with.

Helped in resolving issues related to data integration and UI bugs.

🧰 Technologies Used

HTML & CSS: For page layout and design

JavaScript: For interactivity and DOM manipulation

Responsive Design: Used media queries to make the layout work on all devices

Team Tools: GitHub for collaboration and version control

🌟 What I Learned

Practical experience in real-world front-end development tasks

Enhanced skills in UI design, teamwork, and requirement analysis

Learned how to structure large-scale project tasks and coordinate with a group to meet timelines

✅ Conclusion

This internship not only improved my technical and leadership abilities, but also gave me valuable exposure to real-time project management and frontend development practices. It was a strong foundation for my growth as a future full-stack developer.
